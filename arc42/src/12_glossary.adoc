[[section-glossary]]
== Glossary

[cols="e,2e" options="header"]
|===
|Term |Definition

|Provenance
|The complete history of how a research artifact was created, including code, parameters, environment, and outputs.

|Adapter
|A software component that automatically captures and reports provenance metadata from simulation code.

|Governance
|Ensuring that provenance tracking happens consistently and correctly, driven by convenience rather than enforcement.

|Minimal Friction
|Design principle that integration should require minimal effort from the user.

|Output Agnosticism
|Ability to support various output formats and types without restrictions.

|===
